Betasource Cybersecurity follows a structured approach to provide comprehensive cybersecurity solutions. The following are the typical steps that we follow to ensure that our clients are protected from cyber threats:

Assessment

We thoroughly evaluate the client's IT infrastructure, applications, and data to identify vulnerabilities and potential security risks.

Design

Based on the assessment, we design a customized cybersecurity plan that addresses the client's specific needs.

Implementation

Once the client designs and approves the plan, we start the implementation phase. We deploy hardware and software solutions to secure the client's IT infrastructure, including firewalls, antivirus software, and intrusion detection systems.

Testing

We test the deployed solutions to ensure they work correctly and effectively.

Monitoring

We continuously monitor the client's IT infrastructure for signs of cyber threats.

Maintenance

We provide ongoing care and support to ensure the client's cybersecurity measures are up-to-date and effective.

Reporting

We provide regular reports to the client on the status of their cybersecurity measures. Our reports include detailed information on security incidents, vulnerabilities, potential risks, and recommendations for further improvements.
