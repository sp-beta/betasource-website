So, What are VR and AR, and How Can they Benefit Your Business?

Virtual reality (VR) is a technology that allows users to immerse themselves in a virtual world using specialised hardware such as VR headsets and controllers. With VR, businesses can create immersive training simulations, virtual showrooms, and other interactive experiences that help them engage with their customers and employees in new and exciting ways. On the other hand, augmented reality (AR) is a technology that overlays digital content in the real world using a camera-equipped device such as a smartphone or tablet. With AR, businesses create interactive product demonstrations, virtual try-on experiences, and other engaging content that help them improve customer experiences and drive sales.
